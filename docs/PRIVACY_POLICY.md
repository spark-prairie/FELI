# FELI Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 29, 2025

---

## English Version

### 1. Introduction

FELI ("we," "our," or "the App") is an AI-powered cat emotion analysis application designed for entertainment and educational purposes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

**This Privacy Policy explains:**
- What data we collect
- How we use your data
- How we protect your data
- Your rights and choices

By using FELI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

### 2. Data We Collect

#### 2.1 Photos and Images
- **What we collect:** When you use FELI to analyze your cat's emotions, you provide photos of your cat either by taking a photo with your device camera or selecting one from your photo library.
- **How we use it:** These images are processed by our AI analysis service to detect emotional cues and provide insights.
- **Storage:** Images are temporarily stored on your device for display purposes and are NOT uploaded to our servers or stored remotely unless you explicitly choose to save analysis results.
- **Retention:** You can delete all locally stored images and analysis history at any time from the Settings screen.

#### 2.2 Analysis History
- **What we collect:** Analysis results (detected emotions, confidence scores, behavioral observations, and care suggestions) are stored locally on your device.
- **How we use it:** This data powers your analysis history feature, allowing you to review past results.
- **Storage:** All history data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.
- **Retention:** Limited to your 10 most recent analyses. You can delete all history data at any time from Settings.

#### 2.3 Usage Data
- **What we collect:** Daily usage count (number of analyses performed per day) to enforce free tier limits.
- **How we use it:** This ensures fair usage of our free tier (2 analyses per day for free users).
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device.

#### 2.4 Subscription Data
- **What we collect:** If you subscribe to FELI Pro, subscription status and entitlements are managed by RevenueCat (our third-party subscription management service).
- **How we use it:** To verify your Pro subscription status and unlock Pro features.
- **Third-party processing:** RevenueCat processes this data according to their Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

---

### 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

#### 3.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
- **Purpose:** Manages in-app subscriptions, purchase verification, and cross-platform subscription syncing.
- **Data shared:** Anonymous subscription identifiers, purchase receipts, and entitlement status.
- **Privacy Policy:** https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

#### 3.2 AI Analysis Service
- **Purpose:** Processes cat photos to analyze emotional states using computer vision and machine learning.
- **Data shared:** Cat photos you choose to analyze (sent securely via encrypted HTTPS).
- **Data retention:** Photos are processed in real-time and are NOT stored permanently by the AI service.

---

### 4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:
- **Provide core functionality:** Analyze cat emotions and display results
- **Maintain analysis history:** Store your recent analysis results locally for easy access
- **Manage subscriptions:** Verify Pro subscription status and unlock premium features
- **Enforce usage limits:** Track daily analysis count for free tier users
- **Improve the app:** Understand general usage patterns (no personally identifiable information is collected)

---

### 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties** except as described below:

- **AI Analysis Provider:** Cat photos are sent to our AI service provider for real-time analysis. Photos are not stored permanently.
- **RevenueCat:** Subscription data is processed by RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government request.

---

### 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** All data stored locally on your device is encrypted.
- **HTTPS:** All network communications use encrypted HTTPS connections.
- **No Cloud Storage:** Your cat photos and analysis history are stored locally on your device, not on our servers.
- **Third-party Security:** We only work with reputable third-party services (RevenueCat, AI providers) that implement strong security practices.

---

### 7. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights:

#### 7.1 Access
- View all your analysis history directly in the app's History tab.

#### 7.2 Deletion
- **Delete All Local Data:** Go to Settings > Privacy & Data > "Delete All Local Data" to permanently remove all analysis history, cached images, and app data from your device.
- **Effect:** This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

#### 7.3 Subscription Management
- Manage or cancel your FELI Pro subscription through your device's App Store or Google Play account settings.

---

### 8. Children's Privacy

FELI is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

---

### 9. International Users

FELI is available worldwide. If you access the app from outside the region where our servers are located, your data may be transferred internationally. By using FELI, you consent to such transfers.

---

### 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:
- The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised
- Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification
- Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy

---

### 11. IMPORTANT: Non-Medical Disclaimer

**FELI IS NOT A MEDICAL DIAGNOSTIC TOOL.**

FELI provides AI-generated insights for entertainment and educational purposes only. The emotion analysis results are based on visible behavioral cues and computer vision algorithms.

**FELI is NOT a substitute for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment.**

- Always consult a licensed veterinarian if you have concerns about your cat's health, behavior, or wellbeing.
- Do not rely solely on FELI's analysis for medical decisions.
- FELI cannot diagnose medical conditions, illnesses, or pain.
- The app's insights are probabilistic interpretations and may not be accurate in all cases.

If you notice signs of distress, illness, or unusual behavior in your cat, seek professional veterinary care immediately.

### 2. 我们收集的数据

#### 2.1 照片和图像
- **我们收集什么:** 当您使用 FELI 分析猫咪的情绪时,您可以通过设备相机拍照或从相册中选择照片。
- **我们如何使用:** 这些图像由我们的 AI 分析服务处理,以检测情绪线索并提供见解。
- **存储:** 图像临时存储在您的设备上用于显示,除非您明确选择保存分析结果,否则不会上传到我们的服务器或远程存储。
- **保留:** 您可以随时从设置屏幕删除所有本地存储的图像和分析历史记录。

#### 2.2 分析历史
- **我们收集什么:** 分析结果(检测到的情绪、置信度分数、行为观察和护理建议)存储在您的设备本地。
- **我们如何使用:** 此数据为您的分析历史功能提供支持,让您可以查看过去的结果。
- **存储:** 所有历史数据使用加密存储在您的设备本地。
- **保留:** 限制为您最近的 10 次分析。您可以随时从设置中删除所有历史数据。

#### 2.3 使用数据
- **我们收集什么:** 每日使用次数(每天执行的分析次数),以执行免费套餐限制。
- **我们如何使用:** 这确保了我们免费套餐的公平使用(免费用户每天 2 次分析)。
- **存储:** 存储在您的设备本地。

#### 2.4 订阅数据
- **我们收集什么:** 如果您订阅 FELI Pro,订阅状态和权限由 RevenueCat(我们的第三方订阅管理服务)管理。
- **我们如何使用:** 验证您的 Pro 订阅状态并解锁 Pro 功能。
- **第三方处理:** RevenueCat 根据其隐私政策处理此数据:https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
